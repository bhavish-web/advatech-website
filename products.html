Category 01

Optics

Optical components form the heart of any photonics system. Our optics portfolio spans everything from simple windows and prisms to sophisticated multi-element lens assemblies and precision spectral filters. All components are sourced from leading global manufacturers and meet stringent quality and performance standards suitable for research, instrumentation, and industrial use.

Sub-Categories

1. Plano Optics

Flat-surfaced optical elements used for beam manipulation, wavefront control, and spectral filtering. Precision-polished surfaces ensure minimal wavefront distortion across UV, VIS, and IR wavelengths.

Windows

Optical-grade windows in BK7, fused silica, sapphire, and more

Mirrors

Broadband metallic and dielectric mirrors for UV to NIR

Prisms

Penta, Dove, right-angle, Porro prisms for beam deviation

Beamsplitters

Cube and plate beamsplitters, polarising and non-polarising variants

Diffusers & Screens

Ground glass, engineered, and holographic diffusers

Flats

Reference-grade optical flats for interferometry and calibration

Gratings

Ruled and holographic diffraction gratings, blazed for specific wavelengths

2. Lenses

A comprehensive range of imaging, collimating, and focusing lenses for laboratory and industrial photonics applications, from simple singlets to complex multi-element assemblies.

Simple Convex Lenses

Plano-convex and biconvex singlet lenses for collimation and focusing

Concave Lenses

Plano-concave and biconcave lenses for beam expansion

Doublet Lenses

Achromatic doublets for reduced chromatic aberration

Fresnel Lenses

Compact, lightweight Fresnel lenses for illumination systems

Cylindrical Lenses

Single-axis focusing lenses for line-generating applications

Aspheric Lenses

Diffraction-limited aspheric lenses for high NA applications

Concave Mirrors

Parabolic and spherical concave mirrors for laser cavities

3. Filters

Spectral filtering components for wavelength selection, attenuation, and beam management across the UV, visible, and infrared spectrum.

Long Pass Filters

Transmit wavelengths above a specified cut-on wavelength

Short Pass Filters

Transmit wavelengths below a specified cut-off wavelength

Dichroic Filters

Transmit and reflect different spectral bands simultaneously

Neutral Density Filters

Uniform attenuation across the visible and NIR spectrum

Category 02

Optomechanics

Reliable optomechanical components are the foundation of any stable optical system. Our range includes precision mounts for lenses, mirrors, filters, and prisms, as well as complete lens tube systems for modular beam-path assembly. Engineered for rigidity and fine adjustment, these components ensure repeatable, vibration-resistant performance in the most demanding laboratory environments.

Sub-Categories

1. Mounts

Precision kinematic and fixed mounts for holding optical elements in alignment. Available in imperial and metric standards, with fine-thread adjustment screws for angular and translational positioning.

Lens Mounts

Fixed and adjustable mounts for round lens elements

Mirror Mounts

Kinematic mirror mounts with fine angular adjustment

Filter Holders

Slide-in and rotating filter mounts for spectral filtering

Post & Rod Systems

Breadboard-compatible post, rod, and clamp accessories

2. Lens Tubes

Modular SM-threaded lens tube systems for building enclosed, dust-protected optical assemblies. Compatible with standard optics for compact beam-path construction.

Fixed-Length Tubes

SM05 and SM1 fixed-length tubes in various lengths

Extension Tubes

Adjustable extension and coupler tubes for custom assemblies

Retaining Rings

Threaded retaining rings for securing optics in tubes

Category 03

Fiber Optics

Fiber optic components are essential for routing, splitting, filtering, and managing light in telecommunications, sensing, and laboratory systems. Our fiber optics range covers passive and active devices from leading global manufacturers, suitable for single-mode and multi-mode systems across the C, L, O, and S telecom bands as well as general photonics research wavelengths.

Sub-Categories

Fiber Optic Components

Passive fiber components designed for signal management, wavelength routing, and polarization control in fiber-based optical systems.

Fiber Optical Isolator

Prevents back-reflection into laser sources; low insertion loss, high isolation

Fiber Coupler / Fused WDM / Splitter

Power splitting and wavelength combining/splitting for fiber networks

Isolator Integrated Hybrid Device

Multi-function hybrid devices integrating isolation and coupling functions

CWDM / DWDM / FWDM / AWG

Wavelength division multiplexing components for telecom and sensing

FWDM / Band Pass Filter (BPF)

Fiber wavelength division multiplexers and bandpass filter modules

In-Line Polarizer

Compact fiber in-line polarizers for polarization-sensitive systems

Category 04

Electronics

No photonics lab is complete without robust electronic instruments for driving, measuring, and characterising optical systems. Our electronics range provides the essential instruments needed to operate, control, and diagnose laser, detector, and modulator setups — from benchtop power supplies to high-bandwidth oscilloscopes and precision optical power meters.

Sub-Categories

Lab Instruments & Measurement Equipment

Essential electronic instruments for driving, controlling, and measuring photonic and optoelectronic systems in research and development environments.

Oscilloscope

High-bandwidth digital oscilloscopes for visualising electrical signals from detectors, modulators, and laser drivers

Function / Signal Generator

Arbitrary waveform and function generators to drive modulators, LEDs, and piezo stages

Multimeter (Digital / Analog)

Precision multimeters for measuring voltage, current, and resistance across optical circuit boards and components

Power Supply (DC Bench Supply)

Stable, low-noise DC bench power supplies for lasers, detectors, and driver circuits

Power Meter (Optical + Electrical)

Optical power meters for laser output characterisation and electrical wattmeters for circuit load measurement
